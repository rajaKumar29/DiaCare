Clinical Factors That Influence Blood Sugar

Dia Care evaluates multiple physiological and lifestyle parameters to estimate glucose patterns and support informed medical decisions.

Nutrition & Diet

Food composition, meal timing, and carbohydrate balance directly affect glucose response.

Medication & Insulin

Dosage, timing, and adherence to diabetes medication are analyzed for prediction accuracy.

Physical Activity

Exercise intensity and daily movement influence insulin sensitivity and glucose utilization.

Stress & Emotions

Hormonal stress responses can elevate blood sugar and alter metabolic balance.

Sleep & Routine

Sleep quality and circadian rhythm significantly impact glucose regulation.

Illness & Hydration

Infection, dehydration, and heat stress can disrupt normal glucose stability.
